# Cell Press Journal Style

Cell, Molecular Cell, Developmental Cell, Cancer Cell, Cell Reports, etc.

## Typography
- Font: Arial or Helvetica Neue
- Title: 11pt bold
- Axis labels: 9pt regular
- Tick labels: 7pt regular
- Legend: 8pt regular
- Text color: dark gray (#333333), not pure black

## Colors
- Cell Press allows more colorful figures than Nature/Science
- But still use restrained, professional palette
- OK to use distinct colors for different data categories
- Avoid neon/bright colors

## Layout
- Aspect ratio: varies by figure type
- Axis style: open (classic biology style)
- Ticks: inward, thin
- Background: white
- Gridlines: none by default
- Legend: top-right inside or right outside plot area

## Export
- DPI: 300 for color, 500 for line art
- Format: TIFF, EPS, or PDF
- Width: 85mm (single column), 115mm (1.5 column), 180mm (double column)

## Multi-panel
- Panel labels: uppercase "A", "B", "C" or lowercase "a", "b", "c"
- Consistent lettering style across all panels
- Figure legend: separate, below figure or on facing page

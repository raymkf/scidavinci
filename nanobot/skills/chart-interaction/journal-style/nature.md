# Nature Family Journal Style

Nature, Nature Medicine, Nature Genetics, Nature Biotechnology, Nature Communications, Scientific Reports, etc.

## Typography
- Font: Arial or Helvetica
- Title: 12pt bold, top-left aligned
- Axis labels: 10pt regular
- Tick labels: 8pt regular
- Legend: 9pt regular

## Colors
- Preferred: colorblind-friendly palette
- Avoid: pure red/green combinations (protanopia issue)
- Background: white (#FFFFFF), no gridlines by default
- Data: use the journal palette from the base skill

## Layout
- Aspect ratio: 4:3 or 1:1 preferred
- Axis style: open (L-shape), no top/right borders
- Ticks: inward, 4pt length
- Plot area margins: tight but not crowded
- Legend: inside plot area, top-right preferred

## Export
- DPI: 300 minimum for print, 600 for line art
- Format: TIFF or high-res PNG for initial submission; vector (PDF/EPS) for revision
- Width: 80mm (single column), 170mm (double column)

## Multi-panel
- Panel labels: lowercase bold "a", "b", "c" in top-left of each panel
- Composite figures: use consistent styling across panels
- Include figure legend as separate text (not on figure)

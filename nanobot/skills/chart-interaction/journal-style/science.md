# Science Family Journal Style

Science, Science Advances, Science Translational Medicine, Science Signaling, etc.

## Typography
- Font: Helvetica or Arial
- Title: 11pt bold
- Axis labels: 9pt regular
- Tick labels: 7-8pt regular
- Legend: 8pt regular

## Colors
- Conservative palette, avoid saturated colors
- Preferred: muted tones with good grayscale conversion
- No red/green-only encoding for critical data

## Layout
- Aspect ratio: flexible, often wider than tall
- Axis style: minimal, thin lines (0.5pt)
- Gridlines: none by default; light gray (0.25pt) if needed
- Compact overall; Science figures tend to be data-dense

## Export
- DPI: 300 for halftone, 600-1200 for line art
- Format: EPS/PDF for vector; TIFF for raster
- Width: 55mm (single column), 115mm (1.5 column), 180mm (double column)

## Text in figures
- Minimize text within the figure itself
- Define all abbreviations in the figure legend
- Panel labels: uppercase "A", "B", "C" in bold
